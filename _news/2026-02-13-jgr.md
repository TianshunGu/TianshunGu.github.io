📄 Our study on the attribution of spatially compounding heatwave–flood events has been published in *Journal of Geophysical Research: Atmospheres* (Nature Index journal).  

This work advances the understanding of compound climate extremes by disentangling the mechanisms driving the co-occurrence of heatwaves and flooding at regional scales.
